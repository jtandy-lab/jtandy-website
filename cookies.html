Cookie Policy

Last updated: 2 March 2025

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember your preferences and understand how you use it. Some cookies are essential for the site to function; others help us improve your experience.

2. Cookies we use

We keep things minimal. Here's a clear breakdown:

Essential cookies

These are necessary for the website to function properly. They cannot be disabled.

Cookie Purpose Duration
cookie_consent Remembers your cookie preference so we don't keep asking 12 months

Analytics cookies

These help us understand how visitors use the site — which pages are popular, where people drop off, etc. No personally identifiable information is collected.

Cookie Purpose Duration
_ga Distinguishes unique visitors (Google Analytics) 2 years
_ga_* Maintains session state (Google Analytics) 2 years

3. Third-party cookies

We don't use any advertising, remarketing, or social media tracking cookies. We don't track you across other websites.

4. Managing cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Here's how for major browsers:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Apple Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

Blocking essential cookies may prevent parts of the site from working correctly.

5. Changes

We may update this policy if we add or remove cookies. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
