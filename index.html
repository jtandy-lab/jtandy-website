Frequently asked questions

Will this annoy customers with too many messages?

No. Every message is carefully designed to feel helpful, not spammy. We follow conservative contact rules — a confirmation, one follow-up, and a review request. That's it. You can adjust everything.

What if we already have a website?

We can either optimise your existing site for conversions or build a new high-performance page alongside it. Most trades see better results with a dedicated lead page, but we'll assess what works for you in the audit.

Can this work with my current phone number and email?

Yes. We wire everything into your existing phone number, email, and tools. No new systems to learn. The automations run invisibly behind what you already use.

How do you measure ROI?

You get a live dashboard that tracks enquiries, response times, follow-ups sent, bookings made, and reviews generated. You can see exactly what the system is doing and what it's worth in real numbers.

How quickly can we launch?

Most systems go live within 7 days. We audit in day 1–2, build in 3–5, test on day 6, and launch day 7. Complex setups may take up to 14 days.
