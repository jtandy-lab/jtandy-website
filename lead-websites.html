Frequently Asked Questions

Why are your sites more expensive than standard web design? +

Standard web design focuses on aesthetics ("brochure sites"). We engineer lead generation systems. Our pricing reflects the structural planning, conversion architecture, and local SEO foundation required to actually turn visitors into paying jobs. A £500 site is an expense; a £3,500 lead site is an asset that pays for itself.

Do I need the automation layer straight away? +

No. We actually recommend starting with the core Lead Website. Once the site is generating a consistent flow of inbound enquiries, you can seamlessly layer on missed-call text-back and quote follow-ups to plug any operational leaks.

Will I lose my current rankings if I switch? +

We handle the technical migration extremely carefully. We map and redirect all your existing URLs to ensure you don't lose any current authority, while the new structure gives you a much stronger foundation to grow your rankings further.
