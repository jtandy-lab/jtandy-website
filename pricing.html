Pricing questions

Can I start with just the website?
Yes. Many clients start with the Lead Website structure (£3,500) and add automation layers once their organic enquiry volume reliably increases.
Is there a contract?
Website builds and setup fees are one-off. Any ongoing automation management is month-to-month. You own your website completely after launch.
Why don't you offer fixed pricing?
Every trade business is different. A builder targeting 3 counties with 8 specific services requires a different architectural scope than a local plumber focusing on one city. We audit your specific situation and provide a fixed quote before any work begins.
