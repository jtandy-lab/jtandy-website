Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2 March 2025

1. Who we are

JTandy Automations ("JTandy", "we", "us") is a sole-trader business registered in the United Kingdom. We build revenue systems — websites, follow-up automation, and review automation — for UK tradespeople.

Data controller: James Tandy
Email: James.Tandy@jtandy.com

2. What data we collect

We only collect data you provide directly:

  • Contact forms: Name, email address, phone number (optional), message or current situation description
  • Audit requests: Business name, website URL, contact details
  • Email correspondence: Any information you share when emailing us

We also collect limited technical data automatically:

  • Analytics: Pages visited, time on site, referral source (via privacy-respecting analytics)
  • Cookies: See our Cookie Policy for details

3. How we use your data

We use your personal data to:

  • Respond to your enquiry or audit request
  • Send you a written recommendation or lead leak report
  • Provide the services you've purchased
  • Improve our website and services

We will never sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Legal basis for processing

Under UK GDPR, we process your data based on:

  • Consent: When you submit a form or contact us, you consent to us processing that data for the stated purpose
  • Contractual necessity: When we need your data to deliver services you've purchased
  • Legitimate interest: To improve our website and understand how visitors use it

5. Third-party services

We use the following third-party services that may process your data:

  • n8n (self-hosted): Workflow automation for form handling and email delivery
  • Email provider: To send you communications
  • Web hosting: To serve this website

All third-party services are selected for their data protection practices and, where applicable, are GDPR-compliant.

6. Data retention

We keep your data for as long as necessary to fulfil the purpose for which it was collected:

  • Enquiry data: Up to 24 months after last contact
  • Client data: For the duration of our engagement, plus 6 years for legal/accounting purposes
  • Analytics data: Aggregated and anonymised; no personally identifiable data is retained

7. Your rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access — Request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Rectification — Ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure — Ask us to delete your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restrict processing — Ask us to limit how we use your data
  • Data portability — Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Object — Object to processing based on legitimate interest

To exercise any of these rights, email James.Tandy@jtandy.com. We'll respond within 30 days.

8. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data, including encrypted connections (HTTPS), secure hosting, and limited access to personal data. No method of electronic transmission is 100% secure, but we do our best.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We won't reduce your rights under this policy without your explicit consent.

10. Complaints

If you're unhappy with how we handle your data, you can contact us at James.Tandy@jtandy.com. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO).
